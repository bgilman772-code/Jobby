Quick Apply — Job Board Login Credentials

Some job boards require you to be logged in before applying. Save your credentials here so Quick Apply can log in automatically and submit applications without interruption.
Credentials are stored locally on your machine only — never sent anywhere.

Which sites typically require login? LinkedIn Jobs, Indeed, ZipRecruiter, Dice, Glassdoor, and Monster require an account.
Greenhouse, Lever, Ashby, and most ATS direct links do not require login — they work out of the box.
